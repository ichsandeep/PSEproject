Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my bike?

Simply create an account, navigate to the listing section, and fill out the form with your bicycle's details.

How long can I rent a bike?

Bikes can be rented for a minimum of one day and a maximum of one week.

Is there a fee for using RentOBike?

No, our service is completely free to use for both renters and owners.

What happens if the bike I rented gets damaged?

As a renter, you are responsible for repairing or replacing the bicycle in case of damage or loss.

How do I resolve a dispute with another user?

While RentOBike does not mediate disputes, we recommend directly communicating with the other party to resolve any issues. If necessary, seek legal advice.
